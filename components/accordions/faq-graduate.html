Graduate Help

Graduate students should begin by following the ASA registration process and submitting any available supporting documentation for review.

ASA staff may follow up to clarify documentation, discuss support needs, or schedule an intake meeting depending on the request.

Documentation requirements depend on the accommodation being requested. In some cases, ASA may accept recent provider documentation, a record of prior accommodations, or other supporting materials.

If documentation is still pending, ASA staff may explain next steps and whether provisional review is possible.

For general questions, contact asa@sbu.edu.

ASA staff can provide guidance on documentation, intake expectations, and next steps in the accommodation process.
