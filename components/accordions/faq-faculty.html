Faculty Help

Faculty will receive accommodation notifications after ASA staff review and finalize the student request. In this prototype, those records appear in the Accommodation Letters tab of the faculty dashboard.

Letters are routed through ASA first, then prepared for faculty release as part of the office workflow.

Student exam-related activity appears in the Exam Requests tab, while submitted testing materials appear in the Uploaded Exams tab.

Faculty exam delivery details and related setup preferences can be reviewed in the Exam Preferences tab.

For general ASA questions, contact asa@sbu.edu.

For testing-related questions, contact asaexams@sbu.edu.
