Student Help

Start by completing the student registration form. Once your registration is submitted, ASA staff can review your information and begin the intake process.

If documentation is available, you may upload it during registration. If you are still working on obtaining documentation, you can acknowledge that it is still needed and continue the process.

Use the Accommodation Letter Request form to request letters for your instructors.

Use the Exam Scheduling Request form to request an exam appointment through the ASA testing process.

You can review updates through the student dashboard tabs for accommodation letters, exam requests, documentation, and request status.

For general questions, contact asa@sbu.edu.

For testing-related questions, contact asaexams@sbu.edu.

You can also schedule a meeting with staff using the student action links provided in the portal sidebar.
