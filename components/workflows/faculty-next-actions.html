Faculty workflow

What ASA may need from you

Start by selecting a course. Then review accommodation letters, respond to exam requests, upload exam materials, and set exam preferences for that course.

Step 1

Select a course

Course selection unlocks the letters, exam requests, uploaded exams, and preferences tabs.

Step 2

Review letters

Confirm which students have active accommodation notices for the selected course.

Step 3

Respond to exam requests

Provide exam delivery instructions, approved time, duration, and return method.

Step 4

Upload or verify exam files

Make sure ASA has the current exam materials and any handling instructions.
